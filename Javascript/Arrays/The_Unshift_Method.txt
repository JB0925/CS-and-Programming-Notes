====== The Unshift Method ======
Created Thursday 18 March 2021

Similar to Python's **lst.insert(0, 'xyz') **or **deque.appendleft('xyz')**.

**Example:**
	**var myList = [1,2,3];**
	**myList.unshift(0);**
	**console.log(myList)**
		returns **[0,1,2,3]**
