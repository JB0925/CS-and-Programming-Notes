====== Escape Sequences in Strings ======
Created Tuesday 16 March 2021

**What can you escape out of in a string?**

**\' - single quote**

\" - **double quote**

\\ - **backslash**

\n - **newline**

\r - **carriage return**

\t - **tab**

\b - **backspace**

\f - **form feed**
