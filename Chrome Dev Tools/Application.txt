====== Application ======
Created Thursday 09 December 2021

The Application tab allows you to look at localStorage, sessionStorage, and more.

To view either one:
	- click **application tab**
	- click Local Storage, etc.
	- click the URL
	- to the right, you'll see what's in the storage

Click **cookies**, and then the url, to view your **cookies.**
