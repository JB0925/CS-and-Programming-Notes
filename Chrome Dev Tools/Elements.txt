====== Elements ======
Created Thursday 09 December 2021

**Three ways to access an HTML element in the "elements" tab:**

1). By going into the elements tab itself, and clicking and highlighting the element you want.

2). By going to the element itself and clicking CMD + click, then **inspect**. This will take you directly to the element in the elements tab.

3). By clicking the **Arrow in the box** that is directly beside the **responsive design icon.**

**Element good to knows:**
	- lightish green: the amount of margin persent
	- orangish: the amount of padding present
