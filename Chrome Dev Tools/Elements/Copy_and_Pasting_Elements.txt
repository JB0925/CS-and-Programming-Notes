====== Copy and Pasting Elements ======
Created Friday 10 December 2021

To copy and paste an element:

1). Select the element.

2). CTRL + click it, and click **copy -> copy element**


**To Paste:**

1). Click the container in which you want to place the new element.

2). CTRL + click it, and then click **paste**.
