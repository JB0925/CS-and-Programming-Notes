====== Toggling classes ======
Created Friday 10 December 2021

To look at an element's styles, select that element and look below to see what styles are given to it.

**To toggle a class on/off of an element, look to the TOP of the STYLE box. You can click on and off of the blue arrows to toggle classes.**
