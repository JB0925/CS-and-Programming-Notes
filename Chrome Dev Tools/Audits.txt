====== Audits ======
Created Saturday 11 December 2021

The **lighthouse** tab gives you ratings as to the performance of your website.

click it, click off of **progressive web app**, then click **generate report.**

To run another report on a different page, look next to the lighthouse in the top left, and then click the down arrow. Finally, click **new report.**
