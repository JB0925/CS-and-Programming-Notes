====== TypeCasting Variables ======
Created Sunday 12 December 2021

**Example:**

const number2: **number** = 5;
const isHappy: boolean = true;


**You can also typecast variables ahead of time:**

**Example:**
	let number1: **number**
	
	number1 = 5;
	
	number1 = "12" **// will throw error**
