====== Stopping an EC2 Instance ======
Created Sunday 09 January 2022

To stop an EC2 instance, from the **Instances** dashboard, you can either click **control + click** on the **name** or **instance_id**, then scroll down to **stop instance**
	OR
You can click **Instance state** at the top and then click **stop instance**
