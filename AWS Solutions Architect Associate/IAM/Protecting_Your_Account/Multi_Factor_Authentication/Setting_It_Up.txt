====== Setting It Up ======
Created Saturday 08 January 2022

In the dashboard, click your username, then click **security credentials.**

Scroll down, and click **Assign MFA Device.**

Click **virtual MFA** and then click **show QR code.**

Scan it with Google Authenticatior, then enter the six digit code twice.
