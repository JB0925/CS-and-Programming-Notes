====== How To ======
Created Saturday 08 January 2022

In the IAM dashboard, under **users** on the left, click **Roles.**

Click **Create role**

**Common Uses for Roles:**
	- EC2s
	- Lambda (allow lambda fns to call AWS on your behalf)
	

**To Add to EC2:**
	- Click on EC2
	- In the bottom right, click on **Next:Permissions**
	

From there, you can either add / search for existing policies to add to the role, or you can create custom policies to add to the role.
