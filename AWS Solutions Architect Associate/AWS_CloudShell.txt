====== AWS CloudShell ======
Created Saturday 08 January 2022

CloudShell is like a VM on AWS that is only available in certain AWS Regions.

you can do all kinds of things with cloudshell, including everything that you can do in a a local terminal.

You can download files from CloudShell to your local machine, and you can also upload files from your local machine to access within CloudShell.
