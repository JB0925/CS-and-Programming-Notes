====== The Pen Tool ======
Created Monday 09 August 2021

Use the pen tool (looks like a pen) to draw shapes / vectors.

Hold **shift** to be able to keep the line straight.

Afterward, you can go over to **fill**, **stroke, effects** to alter the way the shapes look.
