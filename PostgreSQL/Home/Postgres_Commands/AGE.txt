====== AGE ======
Created Thursday 04 March 2021

You can get the age of a person, or the difference in time between now and when something was created, by using **AGE.**

{{.\pasted_image.png}}

- first one returns a column of the ages of the people
- second is similar, but returns more columns

**AGE()** **takes two parameters; usually NOW()** **and some date in the past.**
