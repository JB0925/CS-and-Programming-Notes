====== Extracting Fields ======
Created Thursday 04 March 2021

**EXTRACT() **allows you to pull out one aspect of a SQL statement, i.e. a year, instead of the entire date

**SELECT EXTRACT(YEAR FROM NOW());**
	- returns the current year
	- can do the same thing with month, date, DOW (day of week), CENTURY,
