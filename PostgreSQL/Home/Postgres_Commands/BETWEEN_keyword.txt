====== BETWEEN keyword ======
Created Wednesday 03 March 2021

The **BETWEEN **keyword is used to select data from a range.

**Example:**
	**SELECT * FROM person**
	**WHERE date_of_birth**
	**BETWEEN DATE '2000-01-01' AND '2015-01-01';**
	
	The above gets all rows and columns for people born between those two dates
