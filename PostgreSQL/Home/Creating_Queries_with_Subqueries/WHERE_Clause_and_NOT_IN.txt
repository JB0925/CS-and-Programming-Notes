====== WHERE Clause and NOT IN ======
Created Wednesday 05 May 2021

{{~/Desktop/ScreenShots/NOT IN statment WHERE clause.png}}

**NOT IN** gives us a way to use a WHERE clause to filter for things that aren't within a particular group.


In this example, we are selecting the department column from the products table, and then we run a subquery to **exclude departments that also have an item with a price less than 100.**
