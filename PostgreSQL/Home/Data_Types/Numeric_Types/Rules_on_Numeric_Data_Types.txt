====== Rules on Numeric Data Types ======
Created Tuesday 18 May 2021

**If the column is the ID column of the table → **Mark the **type** as **serial**

**Need to store a number without a decimal? **→ Mark the **type **as **integer**

**Need to store a number with a decimal, and the data needs to be very accurate? → **Mark the **type** as **numeric**
							think bank balance, grams of gold, scientific calculations, etc.

**Need to store a number with a decimal and the decimal isn't super important?  → **Mark the **type **as **double precision**
							think kilograms of trash in a landfill, liters of water in a lake, air pressure in a tire
