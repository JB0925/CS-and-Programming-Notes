====== Four Different Types of Relationships ======
Created Sunday 28 March 2021

There are four different types of relationships in SQL. They are:
	- **One to many**
	- **Many to one**
	- **One to one**
	- **Many to many**
	
**For examples of One to Many and Many to One, see the two pages with the same names.**
