====== Many to Many ======
Created Sunday 28 March 2021

**Examples of Many-to-Many Relationships:**
{{.\pasted_image.png}}


Students have many different classes, not all are the same. Classes have many different students, not all are the same.

Movies have many different actors and actresses; not all are the same. Actors and actresses are in many different movies, not all of which are the same.
