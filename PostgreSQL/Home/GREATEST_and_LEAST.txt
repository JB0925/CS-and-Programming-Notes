====== GREATEST and LEAST ======
Created Monday 17 May 2021

**GREATEST** takes a list of values and determines which one is the greatest.

**Example:**
	**SELECT GREATEST(weight * 2, 30)**
	**FROM products;**



**LEAST** takes a list of values and determines which one is the least.

**Example:**
	**SELECT LEAST(price * 0.5, 300)**
	**FROM products;**
