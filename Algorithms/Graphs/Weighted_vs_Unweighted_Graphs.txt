====== Weighted vs Unweighted Graphs ======
Created Friday 06 August 2021

**Weighted Graph**
	- a **weighted graph** just means that its **edges**, or connections, have a value


**Unweighted Graph**
	- an **unweighted graph** just means that its **edges**, or connections, have no values
