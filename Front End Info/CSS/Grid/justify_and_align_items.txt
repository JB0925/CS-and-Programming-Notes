====== justify and align items ======
Created Wednesday 12 May 2021

**justify-items: start, center, end**
	- **tries** to sqeeze the content to the specified location **on the row / horizontal axis.**


**align-items: start center, end**
	- same as above, but tries to squeeze them in on the **column / vertical axis**
