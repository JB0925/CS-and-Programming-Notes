====== grid-template-rows ======
Created Monday 10 May 2021

**grid-template-rows** sets the **height** of the rows

{{~/Desktop/ScreenShots/Grid-Rows-and-Columns.png}}

The above has the following settings:
	- **grid-template-columns: 100px 100px 100px**
	- **grid-template-rows: 50px 50px**
	
	So, three rows and two columns.
