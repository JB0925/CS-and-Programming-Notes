====== justify and align self ======
Created Wednesday 12 May 2021

Just as in flexbox, you can use **justify-self** and **align-self** to move one individual item around.


Remember:
	**justify-self = row axis**
	**align-self = vertical axis**
	
	options = **start, center, end**
