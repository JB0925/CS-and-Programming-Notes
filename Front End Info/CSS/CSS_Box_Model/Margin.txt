====== Margin ======
Created Monday 15 February 2021

Margin is the space between the border of one object, and the border of another object.

Just like padding, margin has individual selectors, as well as a shorthand method.

**Individual:**
	- margin-top
	- margin-bottom
	- margin-left
	- margin-right

**Shorthand:**
	- margin
