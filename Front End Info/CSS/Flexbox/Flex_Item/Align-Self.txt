====== Align-Self ======
Created Wednesday 05 May 2021

Allows you to override some of the container properties and adjust individual elements.

{{~/Desktop/ScreenShots/Align-Self.png}}

In div2, the **align-self** property is set to **flex-end.**


You can also use **flex-start (**does the opposite), **stretch**, which stretches the entire viewport on the cross-axis.
