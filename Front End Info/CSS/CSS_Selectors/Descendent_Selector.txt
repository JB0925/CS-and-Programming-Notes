====== Descendent Selector ======
Created Wednesday 10 February 2021

**Descendent Selectors are ones that are nested in, or descend from, other selectors.**

**Example:**
	
	**li a {**
	   **color: teal;**
	**}**

This selects all **<a>** tags that are nested, or are **descendents** of an **<li>** tag.
