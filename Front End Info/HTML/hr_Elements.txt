====== hr Elements ======
Created Friday 05 February 2021

**What is <hr>?**

	- the tag has no closing tag, and no content goes inside of it
	- represents a line break, and is sometimes used to convey a change or diversion from one theme to a new one
	- prints a thin, gray line on screen, though you can style it with CSS
