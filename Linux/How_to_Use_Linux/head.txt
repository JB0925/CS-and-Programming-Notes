====== head ======
Created Saturday 15 May 2021

opposite of **tail**
	
	**head <filename>**
		- returns first 10 lines of a file
	
	**head -<number> <filename>**
		- returns first **<number>** lines of a file
		
		**NOTE: Can also be used with "tail"**
