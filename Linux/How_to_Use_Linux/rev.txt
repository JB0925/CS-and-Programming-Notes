====== rev ======
Created Sunday 16 May 2021

**rev** reverses all of the letters in each word of a text.

**NOTE: Line order is unchanged.**


**Example:**
	EXAMPLE FILE:
		- red
		- yellow
		- green
		
	**rev <filename>**
	
	OUTPUT:
		- der
		- wolley
		- neerg
