====== mmin ======
Created Thursday 03 December 2020

**mmin - 10**

the above can be used in conjunction with other commmands to find out what happened in the last ten minutes

**Example:**

**find . -type f -mmin -10**
	returns a list of the files that were modified in the last ten minutes
