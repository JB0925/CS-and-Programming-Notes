====== Arithmetic Expansion ======
Created Wednesday 26 May 2021

Very simply, arithmetic expansion allows you to perform mathematical operations in the Terminal.

**Syntax:**
	**$((number operator number))**
	**$((3 + 7))		returns 10**
	**$((3 ** 10))	returns 30**
	
	
**List of Operators:**
	multiply = *
	exponentiation = **
	addition = +
	subtraction = -
	division = /
