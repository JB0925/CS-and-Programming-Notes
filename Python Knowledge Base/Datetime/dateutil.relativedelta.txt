====== dateutil.relativedelta ======
Created Monday 21 September 2020

**from dateutil.relativedelta import relativedelta**

https://dateutil.readthedocs.io/en/stable/relativedelta.html

useful as an alternative to datetime.timedelta

pass in two datetime objects and it will find the amount of difference in the years, months, days, etc. Very expansive and will take many arguments.
