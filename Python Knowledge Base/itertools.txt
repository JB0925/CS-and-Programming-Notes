Another example of itertools.count:

**import itertools**


**class Animal:**
**    _seq = itertools.count(10001)**
**    _zoo = []**

**    def __init__(self, name):**
**        self.id = next(self._seq)**
**        self.name = name.title()**
**        self._zoo.append(self)**
