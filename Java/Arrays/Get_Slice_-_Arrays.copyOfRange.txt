====== Get Slice - Arrays.copyOfRange ======
Created Monday 21 February 2022

To get a slice of an array, such as **myArr[3:6]**, or **myArr.slice(3, 6)**, in Java you use:

int[] myNewArray = Arrays.copyOfRange(myArr, 3, 6);
