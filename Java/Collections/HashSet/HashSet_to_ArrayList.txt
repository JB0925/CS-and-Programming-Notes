====== HashSet to ArrayList ======
Created Saturday 26 February 2022

**Example:**

HashSet<String> myHashSet = new HashSet<String>();
myHashSet.add("hi");
myHashSet.add("hello");

**List<String> myArrayList = new ArrayList<String>(myHashSet);**
