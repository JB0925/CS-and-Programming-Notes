====== Size of Primitive Types and Width ======
Created Monday 07 February 2022

A **byte **occupies 8 bites and has a **width** of 8.

A **short** occupies 16 bites and has a **width** of 16,

An **int **occupies 32 bites and has a **width** of 32,

A **long** occupies 64 bites and has a **width** of 64.
