====== Multiple Upper Bounds ======
Created Wednesday 23 February 2022

**Example:**
	public class Team<**T extends Player & Coach & Manager**> {
		**//**
	}
	
	
	Now, you can accept people on the team from the Player, Coach, and Manager class.
